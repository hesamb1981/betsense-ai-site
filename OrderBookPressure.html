OrderBook Pressure Index

OrderBook Pressure Index measures buying and selling pressure across your entire football book and turns it into a simple set of risk indicators.

Inputs

  • Ticket flow and stake size per selection.
  • Live price movements from primary feeds.
  • Shop-level and region-level exposure.

Outputs

  • Pressure score per market and event.
  • Suggested odds moves and limit changes.
  • Heat-maps for traders and management.
