OrderBook Pressure Index

OrderBook Pressure Index measures buying and selling pressure across your entire football book and turns it into a compact stack of risk indicators – so risk and trading teams can see where exposure is quietly building before it shows up in P&L.

Coverage: full football book
Granularity: market · fixture · region
Mode: live & pre-match

Inputs feeding the index

  • Ticket flow and stake size per selection – shop, region and digital.
  • Live price movements from primary feeds and internal odds moves.
  • Shop-level and region-level exposure vs liability thresholds.
Typical data cadence
Live polling
Every 3-10 seconds
Backfill depth
Last 24–72 hours
Regions
Multi-shop · multi-country

Outputs surfaced to desks

  • Pressure score per market, fixture and region – normalised so teams can rank risk in seconds.
  • Suggested odds moves and limit changes when pressure crosses configured bands.
  • Heat-maps for traders, risk leads and management – showing where flows and exposure are no longer in balance.
Where desks actually use it
  • Early-warning layer for in-play and pre-match risk sweeps.
  • Board and management packs: shop pressure vs digital exposure.
  • Audit trail of “what we knew when” on busy trading nights.
