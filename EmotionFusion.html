Why emotion belongs in the stack

In football, crowd behaviour often moves before price and even before the xG graphs. Emotion Fusion Engine is built to surface that early signal without sending false alarms every time there is a loud chant.

The model learns each stadium’s own “normal loud” and only marks regime breaks when multiple channels agree something is different.

IP angle

The IP is not speech-to-text or sentiment itself – those are commodities. The protected layer is how BetSense fuses them into a single Emotion Index and storyboard that plugs straight into trading workflows.

Every match can be replayed as an “emotional timeline” that lines up with odds moves and xG swings – a unique asset for both trading and content.
