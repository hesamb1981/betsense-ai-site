OPTION D · OUTPUT FOCUS
How RBS Option D behaves
Option D is tuned for high-signal switching-risk monitoring over a defined sample window. It is designed to surface:
  • SwitchRisk – aggregate risk of regime switching for the selected club.
  • PanicFlip – probability of late panic flips or meltdown phases.
  • ComebackChance – likelihood that the club can stabilize and mount a comeback.
  • Instability profile – internal markers describing how volatile the regime currently is.

In a full BetSense stack, RBS Option D can be fused with NSI, Emotion and OrderBook layers via the Fusion Engine for richer narratives and stacked risk flags.
