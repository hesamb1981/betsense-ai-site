Behavior Deviation Scanner

The Behavior Deviation Scanner tracks live betting patterns and flags abnormal activity at customer, shop and event level in real time.

What it detects

  • Sharp action and syndicate-style patterns on specific markets.
  • Unusual late money close to kick-off.
  • Suspicious multi-shop behaviour across a region.
  • Potential problem-gambling signals (high frequency, chasing losses, etc.).

Outputs

  • Risk scores per ticket, customer and event.
  • Real-time alerts to your risk dashboard or messaging system.
  • Audit logs for compliance and internal investigations.
