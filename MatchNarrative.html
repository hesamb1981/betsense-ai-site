Ultra Match Narrative Engine

Ultra Match Narrative Engine converts raw event data into human-readable narratives, ready for apps, websites, in-shop screens and commentary support tools.

Highlights

  • Generates instant summaries before, during and after each match.
  • Explains turning points, momentum and key tactical changes.
  • Supports multiple languages and tones of voice.

Distribution

  • JSON feed for developers.
  • In-shop “story ticker” for big screens.
  • Embeddable widgets for websites and mobile apps.
