OrderBook Pressure Index

OrderBook Pressure Index measures buying and selling pressure across your entire football book and turns it into a compact set of live risk indicators your traders can actually act on.

Inputs
Ticket flow
Odds velocity
Stake size
  • Ticket flow and stake size per selection across shops, regions and channels.
  • Live price movements from primary feeds, including sudden gaps and ladder jumps.
  • Shop-level and region-level exposure, mapped to competitions and key fixtures.
Outputs
Risk meters
Limit moves
Heat-maps
  • Pressure score per market and event – where the orderbook is leaning too hard one way.
  • Suggested odds moves and limit changes before pressure turns into real P&L damage.
  • Visual heat-maps for traders and management to see pressure clusters across the board.
