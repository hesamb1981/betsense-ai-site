Why this scanner is different

Traditional tools watch prices and volumes. Behavior Deviation Scanner cares about shape and tempo – how the market moves when nothing is wrong, and how that motion breaks when real stress or manipulation enters.

By learning a local baseline per competition and client, the system avoids naïve global thresholds and becomes specific to each book’s real personality.

IP angle

The IP sits in the combination of: behaviour baselining, deviation scoring and the fixed vocabulary of labels and narratives built for risk & exec users.

Instead of generic anomaly detection, BetSense defines a repeatable “When this pattern appears, tell this story” mapping that can be documented, licensed and defended.
