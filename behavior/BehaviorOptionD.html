How Behavior Option D behaves

Option D scans the last 20–50 matches to understand where a club behaves like its usual tactical DNA and where it deviates into unstable regimes.

  • Stability bands — Long-horizon zones where the club stays close to its normal playing template.
  • Deviation spikes — Phases where the system flags tactical drift, panic patterns or unsustainable game states.
  • Switching-risk — Markers where the club is vulnerable to late flips or regime shifts (e.g. sudden collapse, wild comebacks).
  • Narrative stream — A clean text narrative summarising how “in-character” or “off-script” the club has been over the window.

These Option D profiles are designed for trading desks and operators who need a fast mental model of a club’s behavioural risk before going live.
