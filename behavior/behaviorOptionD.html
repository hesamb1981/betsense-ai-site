Behavior Deviation Engine
Option D cockpit

This independent Behavior Deviation console scans long-run match histories to flag recurring behavioral breaks – moments where a club systematically leaves its “normal” pattern under pressure.

What this Option D module does
Standalone Behavior View
  • Detects systematic deviations in pressing, tempo and risk-taking versus the team’s baseline pattern.
  • Ranks clubs by how often they lose structure under stress (late goals, red cards, market shocks).
  • Builds a clean narrative you can show directly to traders, risk teams or shop clients.
  • Designed to plug into BetSense Ultra or to run as a completely independent Behavior Deviation product.
Sample window: last 50–80 league games
Inputs: match flows, events, late-game states
Output: text narrative + risk tags
