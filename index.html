Integrated modules

Four specialised engines designed to work together or stand alone – depending on how your sportsbook operates.

BetSense Engine Core

Quantum-style pricing engine combining xG, live stats and social sentiment to surface value edges in real time.

  • In-play edge detection vs market odds.
  • Configurable risk profiles per league.
  • API endpoints for traders & models.

HBboard Trading

Operator-style dashboard giving your traders a single screen for match edges, exposure and risk alerts.

  • Live edge feed from BetSense Engine.
  • Position & exposure overview per event.
  • Designed for trading rooms & buy-side desks.

Emotion Index Sentiment

Real-time fan-sentiment signal built from social feeds and market micro-movements.

  • Pre-match & in-play sentiment curves.
  • Shock detection around goals, cards & news.
  • Exportable as an alpha feature for quant models.

LiveApe Engagement

Match-day experience layer showing live edges, momentum and fan mood in a language that marketing teams can use.

  • Visual edge story for each fixture.
  • Embeddable widgets for web & apps.
  • Built on the same engine traders trust.

Combine fast, lightweight signals with deeper narrative analysis for any match, market or event. Start with a quick pulse, then unlock a deeper AI summary when you need more context.

2. Choose analysis mode

Quick = fast pulse, ideal for live trading. Deep = richer narrative, ideal for reports and investors.
