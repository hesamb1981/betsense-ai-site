Golden Engine

Golden Engine is a compact prediction and pricing engine designed for small and mid–size betting shops that need professional-grade odds without a full data-science team.

Key capabilities

  • Pre-match odds generation for major football leagues.
  • Simple REST API – plug into any cash-desk or website.
  • Configurable margin and risk limits per shop.
  • Optimised to run on low-cost servers or on-prem boxes.

Typical clients

  • Independent local bookmakers.
  • Regional chains with 5–50 shops.
  • Legacy systems that need a modern pricing engine.
