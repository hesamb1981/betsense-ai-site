LiveData Connect

Bring your own licensed live sports feeds and plug them directly into BetSense Engines. You keep your coverage and rights – we provide the multi-layered decision brain.

How it works
Integration · Overview
BYO Feeds · Emotion · xG · Orderbook · Quantum · Genius

LiveData Connect is designed for operators and data providers who already own or license live sports data across multiple competitions. Instead of replacing your feeds, BetSense sits on top of them – adding emotion, tempo, xG and orderbook intelligence in real time.

  • You keep your existing data rights, coverage and providers.
  • BetSense Engines consume your feeds via API or WebSocket.
  • Genius / Emotion / xG / Quantum layers return tradable signals and risk flags.
  • All outputs can be pushed into your internal tools or UIs.
Provider profile
Typical LiveData Connect clients are licensed feed providers or trading desks covering:
  • Top-flight football and major tournaments.
  • High-liquidity in-play markets.
  • At least ~7,500 fixtures per month.
What BetSense adds
  • Emotion + crowd pressure index per match.
  • Shot-by-shot xG layering on top of your event feed.
  • Orderbook & micro-quantum momentum for pre-trade edges.
  • Genius summary signals for quick session decisions.
