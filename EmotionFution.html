Emotion Fusion Engine

Emotion Fusion Engine turns raw behavioural and interaction data into an “emotion index” that explains how your players feel about matches, markets and offers.

Signals used

  • Browsing patterns and dwell time on events.
  • Stake changes after goals, cards and key incidents.
  • Response to promos, boosts and personalised offers.

What you get

  • Emotion score per customer, event and competition.
  • APIs for real-time personalisation and content ranking.
  • Export to your CRM and marketing tools.
