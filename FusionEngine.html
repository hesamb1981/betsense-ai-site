How Fusion Engine actually works

The engine runs four specialist models in parallel – Emotion Index, xG Engine, OrderBook Pressure and Quantum micro-momentum – and compresses their output into a single canonical state per fixture.

Each state describes: edge vs market, shock risk, liquidity comfort and model agreement. That state can be read by humans on the console or consumed directly via API.

Instead of wiring five separate feeds into the trading stack, clients only plug Fusion Engine and choose how aggressively to act on the combined edge score.
